General Order of Conduct for Sunshine Lodge Members


FORWARD

This General Order is issued to provide clear guidance and reaffirm the foundational principles governing our operations. It is imperative to underscore, at the outset, that the directives contained herein are subordinate to and in no way supersede the supreme law of the organization: the Governing Charter. Every provision within this General Order must be interpreted and applied in a manner consistent with constitutional mandates, individual rights as stated by the Governing Charter, and its established legal framework. This order serves to streamline procedures, enhance efficiency, and ensure uniformity, all within the unyielding boundaries set forth by the Governing Charter.

These rules outline the expected conduct and responsibilities for all members of the Clans Club International (Sunshine Lodge). Adherence to these principles is paramount for maintaining discipline, effectiveness, and the honorable reputation of our organization.

Respect For Senior Members

SECTION 1

  • All members will show unwavering respect towards senior members, regardless of hierarchy or position. This respect is fundamental to maintaining discipline and cohesiveness.
  • Greetings: Render appropriate greetings to every (senior member and members alike) as prescribed by established protocols.
  • Addressing Brothers: Always address your brothers, especially senior members, using their proper esteem (e.g., "Sir," "Ave Sir") the latter in an official gathering/Activities. Avoid informal language unless explicitly permitted by the senior member in a non-official setting.
  • Deference in Discussion: When engaged in discussions or debates, a junior member will always allow a senior member to speak first and will listen attentively. Disagreements, if any, will be voiced respectfully, focusing on the issue, not the individual.
  • Professional Demeanor: Maintain an ethical and respectful demeanor in the presence of senior members at all times, both official and non-official settings.

Adherence to Order

SECTION 2

  • Unquestioning obedience to lawful orders is the cornerstone of our discipline and operational success. Failure to obey orders will not be tolerated.
  • Prompt Execution: All orders will be executed promptly, efficiently, and to the best of one's efficiency and ability.
  • Clarity and Confirmation: If an order is unclear, a member issued an order is responsible for seeking clarification immediately. Once understood, the order will be confirmed to the issuing Senior member or an executive in position of authority.
  • No Personal Interpretation: Orders are to be followed as given, without personal interpretation or modification, unless a situation that clearly dictates an immediate and necessary discretion from a member to efficiently follow or perform the order given, which must be reported immediately thereafter.
  • Reporting Completion: Upon successful completion of an order, the efficient member will report its completion to the issuing authority.

Presence at All Official Activities

SECTION 3

  • Attendance and active participation in all official activities, e.g. MEET, are mandatory for every member. These activities are crucial for training, coordination, and cohesion.
  • Punctuality: All members will arrive on time, if not early, for all scheduled official activities, including but not limited to, Meets, training sessions, and ceremonies.
  • Preparedness: Members will arrive at official activities fully prepared, dressing appropriately and befitting, and ready to participate.
  • Active Participation: During official activities, members are expected to be attentive, engaged, and actively participate as required. Distractions and disruptive behavior are strictly prohibited.
  • Reporting Absences: Any unavoidable absence from an official activity must be reported and approved in advance, with a valid reason provided. Unauthorized absence will result in disciplinary action.

Misappropriation of Resources and Abuse of Office

SECTION 3

  • Over-invoicing/Under-invoicing: Inflating the cost of goods or services purchased or deliberately under-reporting revenues due to the Lodge, with the difference being pocketed by corrupt members is a gross misconduct/stammer.
  • Personalizing Lodge’s resources: No member shall misuse Lodge resources or facilities for personal financial gain.

Discussion Points

Maintaining Utmost Efficiency: This order acknowledges the necessity for effective command and control, but draws a clear line between legitimate authority and abusive power. It emphasizes that seniority and position held confers responsibility, not license for personal gain or mistreatment.

Culture of Accountability: The emphasis on reporting procedures and disciplinary action fosters our culture of accountability, where misconduct is not tolerated and individuals are held responsible for their actions.

In closing, please note that this general order does not outline specific disciplinary actions or sanctions for any misdemeanor. All matters requiring action/disciplinary action will be referred to the disciplinary committee and by extension, the Lodge LEK and the LKK, for the purpose of ethical consultations that agrees and conforms with the principles and standards of our community, which will convene to assess the severity of each infraction before any final sanctions or disciplines are determined.
